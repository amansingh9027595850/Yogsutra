Curated Services

Whether you seek a rigorous physical challenge or deep meditative rest, we have a curated practice to support your mental and physical needs.

Popular
🧘‍♂️

Vinyasa Flow

A dynamic, breath-synchronized movement practice. We connect sequences of postures together seamlessly, creating a moving meditation that builds strength, cardiovascular health, and focus.

Intensity: High Duration: 60 / 75 Mins
🌸

Hatha Alignment

Focused on static postures, proper structural alignment, and breath. Perfect for beginners and advanced yogis who wish to cultivate clean alignment, muscle endurance, and mindful awareness.

Intensity: Medium Duration: 60 Mins
🕯️

Restorative Yin

A deeply meditative, slow-paced practice. Postures are held passively for 3 to 5 minutes with props to gently target deep connective tissues, ligaments, joints, and quiet the nervous system.

Intensity: Low Duration: 75 Mins
🔔

Sound Bath & Meditation

A passive sensory healing experience using crystal singing bowls, gongs, and tuning forks. The harmonic frequencies slow brainwaves down, facilitating profound restorative relaxation.

Intensity: Low Duration: 60 Mins
✨

Private Yoga Therapy

One-on-one sessions designed to address specific somatic requirements, physical rehabilitation, chronic back pain, or deep emotional stress under personalized guidance.

Intensity: Custom Duration: 60 / 90 Mins
