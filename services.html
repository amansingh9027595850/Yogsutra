Curated Services

Whether you want an active, strength-building workout or deep, relaxing rest, we have a class that fits your body and mind.

Popular

Hatha Yoga

A classic yoga class focused on holding poses, safe posture alignment, and steady breathing. Great for all levels to build strength, flexibility, and overall fitness.

Intensity: Medium Duration: 60 Mins

Ashtanga Yoga

A dynamic, structured style of yoga that follows a specific sequence of poses. It builds physical strength, stamina, and cardiovascular endurance through breath-synchronized movement.

Intensity: High Duration: 60 / 75 Mins

Therapeutic Yoga

A gentle, healing practice tailored to individuals recovering from injuries, managing chronic pain, or seeking structural alignment. Focuses on safe modifications and restorative poses.

Intensity: Low Duration: 60 Mins

Pranayama & Meditation

A peaceful session focused on ancient yogic breathing techniques (Pranayama) and guided meditation to calm the nervous system, reduce stress, and improve mental clarity.

Intensity: Low Duration: 45 / 60 Mins

Acupressure

A traditional therapy where gentle pressure is applied to specific points on your body. It helps improve blood flow, relieves muscle stiffness, boosts energy, and helps your body heal naturally.

Intensity: Low Duration: 60 / 90 Mins

Sound Healing

A meditative experience where participants are bathed in sound waves produced by instruments like Tibetan singing bowls and gongs to promote deep relaxation and stress relief.

Intensity: Low Duration: 60 Mins

Panchakarma

A traditional Ayurvedic system of relaxing oil massages and cleansing therapies. It is custom-designed to remove body toxins, relieve stress, and restore your natural energy.

Intensity: Low / Medium Duration: Custom
