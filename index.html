The Sanctuary

A Sacred Space for Transformation

Yogsutra Yogshala represents a sanctuary from the frantic pace of modern life. We believe that yoga is not just a form of physical exercise, but a deep science of alignment, healing, and inner peace.

Founded by master yoga teacher Sameeksha Pharasi, our yogshala is dedicated to bringing traditional yoga wisdom, breath control, and structural harmony to the modern practitioner.

Our Core Principles

Guided by Tradition

We honor the roots of traditional Hatha and Ashtanga yoga while weaving modern bio-mechanics and anatomical science into every session.

🧘‍♀️

Asana (Postures)

Intentionally sequencing physical postures to build physical strength, cultivate flexibility, and unlock blockages in the energy channels of the body.

💨

Pranayama (Breath Control)

Using breathing techniques to regulate physical energy, soothe the nervous system, and anchor the wandering mind to the present moment.

✨

Dhyana (Meditation)

Resting in steady silence to transcend analytical thoughts, expanding inner awareness, and awakening pure consciousness.
