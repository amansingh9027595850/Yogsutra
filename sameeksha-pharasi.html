About Sameeksha

Sameeksha Pharasi holds a Master's degree in Yoga Science along with a 200-hour Yoga Certification. She is also a state-level yoga player, passionate about helping people improve their health, flexibility, and inner peace through yoga.

She started practicing yoga to understand how it can heal both the body and the mind. Combining her university studies in Yoga Science with her experience as a state-level athlete, she teaches classes that are both physically safe and mentally calming. She helps you connect simple physical movements with steady breathing.

"Yoga is not just a workout," Sameeksha says. "It is a healthy way of living. When we practice regularly, we build discipline. When we breathe steadily, we find balance. And when our mind is quiet, we find true happiness."

Helping you find strength, balance, and peace in daily life. Start your yoga journey with us.
