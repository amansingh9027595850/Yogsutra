About Sameeksha

Sameeksha Pharasi holds a Master's degree in Yoga Science along with a 200-hour Yoga Certification. She is also a state-level yoga player, passionate about helping people improve their health, flexibility, and inner peace through yoga.

Her journey in yoga began as a scientific quest to understand the complex union of body, mind, and spirit. Combining academic research in Yoga Science with active competitive experience as a state-level athlete, Sameeksha brings a unique, holistic perspective to her classes. She bridges traditional practices with modern physical disciplines to cultivate body control and mental stillness.

"Yoga is not just a daily routine," Sameeksha says. "It is a science of lifestyle. Through dedication and physical control, we find discipline. In steady breath, we find balance. And in the quietness of the mind, we find bliss."

Nurturing discipline, balance, and bliss in daily life. Walk the path of yoga science with us.
