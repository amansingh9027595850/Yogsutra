Our Instructors

Guides of the Practice

Every guide at Yogsutra Yogshala brings years of dedicated study, deep anatomical awareness, and a compassionate heart to support your practice.

Ashtanga & Vinyasa Yoga Teacher

Vikas Bisht

Vikas Bisht is a passionate Yoga Teacher with an M.A. in Yogic Science and a Yoga Alliance Certified RYT-200 qualification. Specializing in Ashtanga Yoga, Vinyasa Yoga, and Yoga Workshops, he blends traditional yogic wisdom with mindful movement to inspire strength, balance, and inner peace. His mission is to guide students on a transformative journey toward holistic health and well-being.

Founder & Lead Yoga Guide

Sameeksha Pharasi

Sameeksha Pharasi is a dedicated Yoga Teacher with a Master’s degree in Yogic Science and a Yoga Alliance Certified RYT-200 qualification. With over 5 years of teaching experience, she specializes in Ashtanga Yoga, Pranayama, and Meditation. Her classes focus on improving physical health, mental well-being, and inner balance through authentic and mindful yoga practices. She is passionate about helping students build strength, flexibility, and a healthy lifestyle through yoga.

Therapeutic Yoga & Acupressure Specialist

Ankit Rawat

Ankit Rawat holds a Master’s degree in Yogic Science and has over 5 years of teaching experience. He specializes in Acupressure Therapy, Ashtanga Yoga, and Meditation. His approach focuses on promoting physical wellness, mental relaxation, and holistic healing through traditional yoga and therapeutic practices.
