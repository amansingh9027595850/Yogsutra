Our Instructors

Guides of the Practice

Every teacher at Yogsutra Yogshala has years of experience, a deep understanding of safe body movement, and a warm heart to help you learn.

Ashtanga & Vinyasa Yoga Teacher

Vikas Bisht

Vikas Bisht is a passionate Yoga Teacher with an M.A. in Yogic Science and a Yoga Alliance Certified RYT-200 qualification. Specializing in Ashtanga Yoga, Vinyasa Yoga, and Yoga Workshops, he combines traditional yoga with safe physical movements to help you build strength, balance, and inner calm. He is passionate about helping students improve their health and feel their best.

Founder & Lead Yoga Guide

Sameeksha Pharasi

Sameeksha Pharasi is a dedicated Yoga Teacher with a Master’s degree in Yogic Science and a Yoga Alliance Certified RYT-200 qualification. With over 5 years of teaching experience, she specializes in strength-building yoga poses, breathing techniques, and meditation. Her classes focus on improving physical health, calming the mind, and finding balance. She loves helping students of all levels build strength and flexibility safely.

Therapeutic Yoga & Acupressure Specialist

Ankit Rawat

Ankit Rawat holds a Master’s degree in Yogic Science and has over 5 years of teaching experience. He specializes in healing pressure therapy (acupressure), traditional yoga poses, and meditation. He focuses on helping your body heal, helping your mind relax, and improving your overall wellness.
